PROJECTS

Chip Design with Reinforcement Learning
Feb 2022 - ongoing
Under Prof. Dip Sankar Banerjee | IIT Jodhpur

We pose chip placement as a reinforcement learning program using the graph netlist of the chip placement floorplan as the input data, containing node data and adjacency information. We then train an agent to optimise the quality of chip placements in order to maximise the power and performance, as well as minimise the area. We also set certain conditions in order to take care of the density and routing congestion, that would improve our results further. Our approach learns from past experience and improves over time. As we train over a greater number of chip blocks, our method becomes better at rapidly generating optimised placements for previously unseen chip blocks.
